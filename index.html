Orange juice is a popular and refreshing beverage that is made from the extraction of juice from oranges. Oranges, which are citrus fruits, are known for their vibrant orange color and tangy, sweet flavor.

Benefits athletes

Orange juice can be a beneficial drink for athletes because it contains a variety of nutrients that are important for supporting physical activity and recovery.

Good for runners

An eight-ounce serving of 100% orange juice provides carbohydrates and is a good source of potassium, an electrolyte, that may provide rehydration and recovery benefits post-exercise. All-natural orange juice provides your body with a significant boost of vitamin C, which is essential for the immune system. It's also rich in potassium - one of the most vital electrolytes that our body needs after a workout. Plus, orange juice is hydrating and contains quickly digestible carbohydrates
